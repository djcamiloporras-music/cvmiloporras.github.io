// 001 — BIOGRAPHY

BUILT IN
THE UNDERGROUND

Camilo Porras began his career in New York City in 2021, cutting his teeth at after-parties and clubs across Brooklyn. What started in the underground quickly expanded — performances in Miami, Washington DC, and across the New York scene earned him recognition that labels couldn't ignore.

He is currently signed to 4 labels — 305Made Miami, Xima Records, Dub Miami and Nightshift Records — a testament to a production style that stands apart and continues to attract industry recognition worldwide. His sound combines a European flow with Latin flavor, a hybrid that has allowed him to share the stage with artists like Harvy Valencia, Fronter, Kofla, Jesse Bravo, Ferra Black, Alberto Dimeo, Gustavo Dominguez, and Amal Nemer.

// 002 — FANDOM FILE

THE LAB

[ INCOMING TRANSMISSION FROM THE UNDERGROUND ]

His fans call him "The Modern Dexter" — and not without reason. Like the boy genius who retreated into a secret lab to build things no one else understood, Camilo Porras disappears into his studio and emerges with productions that feel like they were engineered in another dimension.


The basslines hit at frequencies that bypass logic and go straight to the body. The layering is obsessive — every element placed with the precision of someone who considers music a scientific experiment. His crowd doesn't just dance. They get absorbed. They forget where they are. That's what happens when the lab opens its doors.


No flashy gimmicks. No empty hype. Just deep, relentless sound architecture from a man who treats the DJ booth like a control room — and the dance floor like proof of concept.
